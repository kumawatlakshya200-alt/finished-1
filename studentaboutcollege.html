About Us
Engineering College Bikaner (ECB) is one of the premier engineering institutions in Rajasthan, established in 1995. With a commitment to excellence in technical education, ECB has been producing skilled engineers who contribute significantly to the nation's development.
Our college is approved by AICTE and affiliated to Rajasthan Technical University. We offer undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, emphasizing both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.
The college boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a vibrant campus life that nurtures holistic development of students. Our placement record speaks volumes about the quality of education we provide.
